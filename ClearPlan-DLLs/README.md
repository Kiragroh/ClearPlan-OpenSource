# ClearPlan-DLLs

This folder is used for local build dependencies.

Notes:

- The public starter repository intentionally does not track vendor-specific ESAPI assemblies.
- Place your institution-approved copies of `VMS.TPS.Common.Model.API.dll` and `VMS.TPS.Common.Model.Types.dll` into this folder before building.
- Other third-party DLLs may remain here to simplify local onboarding, depending on your redistribution policy.

If your institution prefers a different dependency strategy, update the project references accordingly.
